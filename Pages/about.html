Hi! I'm Tanvi, a creative technologist currently based in NYC. My creative practice is centered around building games, interactive installations, and weird web experiences primarily as educational escapades.

I am a Rhizome Microgrants 2023 recipient and have spoken about my work at Rhizome, Claremont Colleges, and Parsons School of Design. I am currently working on R&D at Lab @ Rockwell Group.
